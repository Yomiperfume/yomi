Our Story

Welcome to YOMI, where fragrance meets elegance.

Founded in 2023, YOMI was created with a simple vision — to bring the world of premium and luxury perfumes closer to you. We specialize in importing high-quality fragrances directly from Dubai, known globally for its rich, long-lasting, and captivating scents.

At YOMI, we believe that a perfume is more than just a scent — it's a statement of personality, confidence, and style. Every fragrance we offer is carefully selected to ensure it meets the highest standards of quality, sophistication, and uniqueness.

What We Offer

Authentic Imported Perfumes — We source our products directly from trusted suppliers in Dubai to ensure originality and excellence.

Premium & Luxury Collection — Our collection is designed for those who appreciate fine fragrances that leave a lasting impression.

Long-Lasting Scents — Each perfume is chosen for its performance, ensuring you smell great all day.

Why Choose YOMI?

  • Trusted quality from Dubai
  • Carefully curated luxury perfumes
  • Customer-focused service

  • Available in 30ml & 50ml
